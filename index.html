Long-Term Risk-Adjusted Return

We at Transcendental Capital focus on the generation of superior long-term return after risk adjustments through a variety of trading and investment strategies, including Quantamental, Event-Driven, and Dynamic Hedging.

Quantamental

Quantamental refers to a combination of quantitative and fundamental methods.

We focus on assets with strong fundamentals and capture trading opportunities revealed from extensive quantitative analysis.

Event-Driven

Many news, political events and corporate actions create temporary mispricings.

We constantly monitor information flows and use thematic filter to identify trending signals that apply to our portfolios.

Dynamic Hedging

Hedging is to preserve value in extreme market conditions.

We actively manage hedging positions in anticipation of rising uncertainties to limit our risk exposures.
